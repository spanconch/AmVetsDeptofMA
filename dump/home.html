AMVETS

is a leading national veteran's service organization and 501(c)(3) nonprofit.

AMVETS has been serving Veterans since 1945 to enhance and safeguard entitlements for all American Veterans who have served honorably, to improve quality of life for them, their families, and the communities where they live through its leadership, advocacy and services.

AMVETS supports all veterans by utilizing the following programs. The Veterans Affairs Voluntary Service (VAVS) and it is the largest volunteer organization in the federal government. State winners enter into a National competition. Scholarships are offered to our nation's Veterans, Active Duty, Guard and Reserves; their Spouses as well as their Children and Grandchildren. We support veterans outreach efforts with a White Clover Program, which allows us to connect with communities. Boy Scouts of America Program AMVETS is a chartered partner with the Boys Scouts of America, who produce fine citizens, many of them veterans, dedicated family members, and strong community leaders for more than 100 years.
